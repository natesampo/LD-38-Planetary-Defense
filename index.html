Planetary Defense is a game developed by Nate Sampo for Ludum Dare 38. In it, the player
must defend a small planet by aiming a massive weapon on the planet to destroy the alien invaders.


Install for Windows:
https://www.dropbox.com/sh/gdwwtkoxx5m4aoz/AAAWdU3UbJ87Vn8vn-_amKFra?dl=1
(Extract the ZIP and run the 'PlanetaryDefense.jar' file)
